System Overview
What is SAINS AR Hub?
A unified Accounts Receivable subledger purpose-built for Malaysian water utilities, replacing fragmented spreadsheets and manual processes with automated, auditable workflows.
👤

490,000 Accounts

Manages the entire SAINS consumer base: domestic, commercial, industrial, government, and institutional accounts across all Negeri Sembilan branches.

💳

8 Payment Channels

Counter (cash/cheque/card), FPX online banking, DuitNow QR, JomPAY, eMandate direct debit, agent collection, Bayaran Pukal, bank transfer.

📜

LHDN e-Invoice Ready

Full MyInvois integration: B2B individual submission, B2C monthly consolidation, 72-hour cancellation, digital signature (SHA256/RSA).

🏛

SPAN Compliant

Automated KPI generation for SPAN regulatory reporting: collection ratio, billing accuracy, complaints, disconnections.

🧠

Intelligent Collections

AI-driven customer segmentation, risk scoring, early intervention alerts, hardship protection, vulnerability management.

🔒

Full Audit Trail

Every action logged with before/after state. PDPA compliant (IC numbers AES-256 encrypted). Role-based access for 14 distinct roles.

Live Demo — Scenario 3
Dunning Lifecycle & Disconnection
An overdue account escalates through 5 dunning levels, from friendly reminder to physical disconnection via Metis field work order.
1
View existing dunning records
Open Dunning History. Accounts at different dunning levels are visible: Level 1 (14 days overdue, friendly SMS/email), Level 2 (30 days, formal notice), Level 3 (45 days, disconnection warning), Level 4 (60 days, disconnected).
Open Dunning History →
2
Check notifications sent
In the Simulator Dashboard, go to Notifications tab. All dunning emails, SMS, and WhatsApp reminders sent by the system are captured here. In production, these go to real recipients.
3
View a Level 4 account with disconnection
Search Customer Accounts for status "TEMP_DISCONNECTED". These accounts have been physically disconnected via Metis work order. When they pay, AR Hub automatically sends a reconnection work order.
Open Customer Accounts →
Key Message

The 5-level dunning process follows Act 655 (Akta Industri Perkhidmatan Air 2006) requirements. Multi-channel notices (email, SMS, WhatsApp, postal) with configurable thresholds. Hardship-flagged and vulnerable customers have capped dunning per social protection policy. Disconnection work orders are automatically dispatched to Metis, and reconnection is triggered the moment payment clears.

Live Demo — Scenario 4
Bank Statement Reconciliation
Daily bank statements arrive via SFTP. AR Hub parses MT940/CAMT.053 files, auto-matches transactions to customer payments, and flags unmatched items for Finance review.
1
Generate a sample bank statement
In the Simulator, go to Bank Statements tab. Select "Maybank", today's date, 5 transactions. Click "Generate MT940". This creates a realistic MT940 file with payments matching actual SAINS accounts.
Open Simulator →
2
View the generated MT940 content
The response panel shows the raw MT940 file with SWIFT-standard formatting: opening balance, credit entries for each payment, closing balance. This is exactly what Maybank deposits on SFTP daily.
3
View Bank Statement Imports
Open Bank Statement Imports in the AR service. Shows imported statements with matched/unmatched counts, auto-match confidence scores, and reconciliation status.
Open Bank Statements →
Key Message

Bank reconciliation runs 3x daily (08:00, 12:00, 18:00). Supports both MT940 (current SWIFT standard) and CAMT.053 (ISO 20022 future standard). Auto-matching by account reference eliminates hours of manual matching. Finance only reviews the exceptions.

Live Demo — Scenario 5
e-Invoice (LHDN MyInvois)
Commercial invoices are automatically submitted to LHDN MyInvois. B2C domestic invoices are consolidated monthly. Full compliance with the Malaysia e-Invoice mandate.
1
View e-Invoice submission batches
Open e-Invoice Submission Batches. Shows batches with status (Preparing, Submitted, Fully Accepted, Partially Accepted, Rejected) and LHDN submission UIDs.
Open e-Invoice →
2
View invoices with e-Invoice status
Open Invoices and filter by einvoiceRequired = true. Commercial/industrial invoices show e-invoice status: SUBMITTED, ACCEPTED (with LHDN UUID), or HELD_NO_TIN (buyer TIN not verified).
Open Invoices →
3
72-hour cancellation window
Each accepted e-invoice has a cancellation deadline (72 hours from LHDN validation). After this window, only a credit note can reverse it — enforced by the system.
Key Message

SAINS serves ~490,000 accounts. B2B invoices (commercial, industrial, government) require individual e-invoice submission with verified buyer TIN. B2C domestic invoices are consolidated monthly (due by 7th of following month). The system handles all this automatically — Finance only manages exceptions and the HELD_NO_TIN queue.

Key Message

Complete financial controls: every ringgit is tracked from invoice to payment to GL. Daily batch posting at 02:00 MYT aggregates all transactions. Debit-credit balance validation prevents posting errors. The audit trail captures who approved what, when, and why.
